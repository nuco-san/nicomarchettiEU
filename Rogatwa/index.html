Rogatwa

Rogatwa is a tabletop game for 4 players, who play as a voodoo priest in the city of New York. The goal of the game is to collect magical items from the garbage cans of the metropolis, which have to be offered to the voodoo spirits, called lwa. The city acts against the players, for the voodoo religion is very unpopular, so they need to dodge the lights and move in the darkness. The game is inspired by Presenze ➙, a book by Francesco Ronzon.

My project team was Davide Ciadamidaro, Matteo Dell'oro, Alessandro Ferrari ➙, Federico Gambarana and Luca Mazzeo ➙.

Designed in 2018.
