Linea Grafica

An unpublished issue of Linea Grafica, a classic italian magazine dedicated to visual communication, was born in the context of a six-month long lab at Politecnico di Milano. Not being published for several years, we were given the mission to re-contextualise the magazine in the modern times. We collected and created visual and written material for the 10 articles of the issue, whom theme was "metamorphosis".

My project team was Mariana Gomez, Luca Sommariva, Matteo Maggi ➙, Arianna Meroni ➙ and Marco Paris ➙.

Designed in 2017.

☟

The magazine contains the interview we did with the Milan-based visual design studio La Tigre ➙.

The magazine was printed with a laser printer on white rough paper and bound as a paperback.
