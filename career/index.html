Who I am

Nicolò, 28yo designer based in Milan.

I was born and raised near Mantua, in a town by the Po river, in 2014 I moved to Milan to attend the Communication Design course at Politecnico di Milano, and I've been living here ever since.

Since 2019 I have been working as visual designer both as freelancer and with award-winning creative studio We Are Müesli, where I have the opportunity to work at both commercial games and applied projects, mostly developed for cultural and artistic contexts.

Between 2020 and 2021 I was an academic tutor for the Game Design department at SAE Institute Milano, where I experienced working in the education field, both inside and outside of the classroom.

What I do

I create visual identities for digital and printed projects, trying to find new, compelling ways of telling stories and convey moods through images and layouts.

I design UIs and interactive experiences, applying user experience and game design concepts to digital interfaces and physical installations.

I create video games as a means of artistic expression, as they allow me to experiment with game design, writing, illustration, animation and coding. In 2023 I founded the video game collective Villadelfina with friend and fellow visual artist Alessandro Ferrari and released our first video game made with Godot Engine.
