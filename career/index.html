About me

I'm currently collaborating as visual designer with award-winning game design studio We Are Müesli, where I have the opportunity to work at both commercial games and applied projects, mostly developed for cultural and artistic contexts.

I also work as a freelance graphic designer, for both corporate branding projects (CO-OP) and visual identities for game-related events (IVIPRO DAYS).

Between 2020 and 2021 I worked for one year as academic tutor for the Game Design department at SAE Institute Milano, where I experienced working in the education field, both inside and outside of the classroom.

In 2023 I founded the video game collective Villadelfina with friend and fellow visual artist Alessandro Ferrari and released our first video game made with Godot Engine.

I completed my master degree in Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano in 2020 with a thesis about the virtual representation of media inside video games and their role as narrative and gameplay tools, which scored a second place in 2020 Archivio Videoludico Award for the best game-related thesis of the year, and that was published in an abriged version as an article.
