Roviza

The reconstruction of the screws brand Roviza goes way back in time. Originally a soviet factory for submarine parts production, Roviza was adapted to the modern marketplace with a unique and very peculiar look. With its collage-mascots and its motto "Keeps things together", Roviza brings high quality screws into every supermarket. In order to strenghten the connection with the user, we designed and developed the interactive machine and videogame Roviza: Screw the Fighter.

My project team was Elena Filippi, Sara Lavazza, Matteo Maggi and Pasquale Morelli.

Designed in 2017.
