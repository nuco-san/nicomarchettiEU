Ventiquattro Elle

Ventiquattro Elle is an escape room inspired by Gianni Rodari's book titled "C'era due volte il barone Lamberto", designed for the 101st anniversary of the author's birth and hosted by Verbania's public library. Through this escape room we wanted to continue Rodari's story, and we imagined for Lamberto, the protagonist, a career in the Circus. The nine puzzles found in the game all take inspiration from professions like "The Origami Tamer" or "The Spoon-thrower".

A project by We Are Müesli.
I was in charge of the visual design of the room's interiors, of the props, and of the desktop application.

Designed in 2020 - 2021.

Scroll down to see the project

The escape room features a ragged band of criminals called "Ventiquattro Elle", who seek the help of the players in order to steal Lamberto's treasure.

For every one of the Circus professions depicted in the game we designed an illustration that was later printed on a large surface inside the room.

The players interact with the game through a desktop application that handles the pacing of the game.
