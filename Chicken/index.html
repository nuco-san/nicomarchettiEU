Space Chicken

A world hanging in the void, a bioluminescent pistachio liquid, a metal hen with a tree on her head. This is what you find watching 3D Space Chicken, an animation made entirely in Blender, following the trip of the weird fluid inside the guts of the chicken. What will be the effects on her organism?

My project team was Marcello Maffi and Matteo Maggi.

Created in 2017.

Scroll to see the project

The background music is Prophecies by Philip Glass.

The chicken was modelled, rigged and animated from scratch.

We used particle systems both for the "limbo" that is the location for the animation and for the flower blooming sequence.
