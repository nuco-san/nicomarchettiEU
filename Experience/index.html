Experience Vault

We collected anonymous users' personal experiences with hallucinogens from the website Erowid, and we crafted a booklet that merges typography and illustration. For every story, I have drawn a scene inspired by the experiences of the narrator.

My project team was Annunziato Mazzaferro and Davide Montesano.

Printed in 2017.

Scroll to see the project

The illustration for the cover flows from front to back.

We tried to merge text and illustration as much as possible, without overlapping them.

Every illustration tries to capture a specific moment described in the story, in this case a person looking at a digital screen.

Experience Vault was sold in 2017 at Fruit Exhibition in Bologna
