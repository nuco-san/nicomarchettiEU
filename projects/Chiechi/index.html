Chi è Chi

Chi è Chi is an interactive game book that was conceived as a printed version of the live escape room Wer Ist Wer, with the goal of making the narrative and the puzzles of the game accessible and playable from home, a challenging goal that forced us to find a way to translate a live experience into printed matter.
The physical book is available on Amazon and is supported by a web application that acts as a game master for the experience.

Together with Wer Ist Wer, Chi è Chi was nominated in the 2021 ADI Design Index catalogue.

A project by We Are Müesli.

I took care of the layouts of the book, the UI design of the web app and the creation of the video trailer.

Printed in 2021.

Scroll down to see the project

Like the escape room version, the players are asked to witness the interrogation of nine characters and then to judge their behaviour according to a set of rules.

The solution for the puzzles have to be verified through the web application we developed to support the experience.
