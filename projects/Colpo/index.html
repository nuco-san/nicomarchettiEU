Colpo Di Stato

Colpo di Stato (Coup d’état) is a card game of historical investigation where you play as a reporter looking into one of the most disturbing yet little-known Italian mysteries: 1970’s attempted “Borghese Coup”.

Colpo di Stato is inspired by escape game mechanics: players collaborate to solve a set of observation, logic and deduction puzzles.

{From: Colpo Di Stato on Eppela}

A project by We Are Müesli.
I worked at the visual design of the deck and of the promotional campaign for the crowdfunding (trailer, website).
