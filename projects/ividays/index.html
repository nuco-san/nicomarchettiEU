IVIPRO Days
2021

In august 2021 I was asked to designed the poster for the fourth installment of IVIPRO Days, an event hosted by the Italian Videogame Program where both Italian and international guests share with the audience their experience in creating or supporting the creation of games that promote the territory and heritage.

Due to Covid-19 restriction, the 2021 edition of IVIPRO Days moved online.

I took care of the visual design of the digital assets for the communication of the event.

Designed in 2021.

Scroll down to see the project

The front of the poster is both a reference to one of the most recognizable physical support for old-school videogames, the floppy disk, and an idealized planimetry of a historical Villa (I took Palladio's La Rotonda as a starting point).

The visual aesthetic of the poster was translated into different social media assets, like banners and overlays for the speakers.
